Quick Advance AI Your financial advisor
Should I use my advance for inventory or hire a second employee?
It depends on your margins and timeline. Inventory delivers faster ROI if your product margin is above ~35% — you can potentially generate returns within the same repayment cycle. A new hire typically takes 3–4 months to become revenue-positive. Key question: what does your cash flow look like in months 2–3 of repayment?
What's my actual cost of capital?
With a typical factor rate of 1.25–1.35, your effective annualized cost ranges from 50–75% APR depending on your term length. That sounds steep, but if capital generates more revenue than it costs — it's still a net positive. I can calculate your exact break-even once you're funded.
